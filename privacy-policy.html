Privacy Policy

At Moments.live, your privacy is our priority. This privacy policy outlines the information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have in managing your data. By using Moments.live, you agree to the terms of this policy.

Information We Collect

Moments.live collects the following information when you use our service:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, and any other information you provide when creating an account or using the app.
  • Photos and Videos: Images and videos uploaded to the platform as part of your wedding events.
  • Event Details: Event names, dates, and guest lists you create within the app.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with Moments.live, such as your activities within the app, pages viewed, and engagement patterns.

How We Use Your Information

Moments.live uses your information to:

  • Provide and personalize our service for you.
  • Allow you to create and share wedding moments with your invited guests.
  • Improve the functionality, performance, and user experience of Moments.live.
  • Respond to your inquiries and provide customer support.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

Moments.live does not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties except in the following circumstances:

  • With your explicit consent for specific services or features.
  • When required by law or to protect the safety, rights, or property of Moments.live, our users, or others.
  • To service providers who assist us in maintaining or improving our platform (e.g., hosting services, analytics providers).

Security of Your Data

We are committed to ensuring that your information is secure. Moments.live uses industry-standard security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, disclosure, or alteration. However, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your data.

Your Data Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete the personal information we hold about you.
  • Control your communication preferences and opt out of marketing messages.
  • Request a copy of your data or ask us to delete your account.

To exercise these rights, please contact us at support@moments.live.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Moments.live uses cookies and similar technologies to improve your user experience, remember your preferences, and analyze usage patterns. You can manage your cookie settings in your browser at any time.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. When we make significant changes, we will notify you by posting the updated policy on our website and/or through other communication methods. Please review the policy periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your data.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy, please reach out to us at support@moments.live.
